## Let's Have a Real Conversation

Democratic AI Consulting is not the right fit for everyone. The clients it serves best are professionals who take the question of how they use AI as seriously as whether they use it, who understand that implementation is a strategic decision and not just a productivity play.

If that description fits, reach out. The first conversation is straightforward: what your practice looks like right now, where you think AI could help, and what you are most concerned about getting wrong. From there, the right engagement type becomes fairly obvious.

Capacity is intentionally limited. Every engagement involves the founder directly, which means there is a ceiling on how many active clients the practice takes on at one time. If you are ready to move, sooner is better than later.
